• Tubesearch

    Tubesearch is my YouTube API hack for finding acapellas on YouTube for music production. Pretty straight forward, just enter an artist, song, or both and Tubesearch will return a list of clickable thumbnails. Click the thumbnails to see(hear) the video, and there's an option in Tubesearch to redirect you to a YouTube audio ripping service. Boom. You've got that acapella you wanted to use.
