• Flickeroo

    Looking for a background image? Flickeroo is an easy way to find one. It's an API tweak to only deliver the sizes you specify with "creative-commons" licences. Really simple and straight forward interface, so you can prety much just enter a visual "theme" and go. In case the interface is too complicated and you dont satisfy the search criteria (search term and device size), Flickeroo reminds you about what criteria it needs with a friendly alert.
